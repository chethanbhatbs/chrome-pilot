What it does

Everything you need to tame a hundred tabs

No bloat, no account, no learning curve. Open the panel and it just works.

Profiles & windows

Seamlessly switch between your Chrome profiles and windows, without losing your place.

Group by window or site

Flip between a window view and a clean per-site view that nests tabs under each domain.

Safe duplicate cleanup

Finds genuine duplicates (same page) and closes them in one click, never your other tabs on the same site.

Focus mode

Lock attention to a set of tabs and get gently nudged back, without destroying anything you opened.

Real time-per-site

See actual measured time you spend on each site, tracked locally, never guessed or faked.

Bulk actions

Suspend inactive tabs, mute all audio, restore sessions, and multi-select, all a click away.

No fake numbers

Time tracking that tells the truth

Most "time spent" features multiply your visits by a guess. This one doesn't. It measures the actual time a site is your active, focused tab (and pauses when you're idle), stored entirely on your device.

Visit counts come straight from your browser history. Nothing is invented.

Get started

Install in under a minute

Open source. Load it as an unpacked extension, works on Chrome for macOS, Windows, and Linux.

Download tab-pilot.zip

Download tab-pilot.zip and unzip it.
Open chrome://extensions and turn on Developer mode (top-right).
Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped tab-pilot folder.
Press ⌘/Ctrl ⇧ E to open the panel. That's it.
Private by design. No account, no sign-in, no tracking server. Your tabs, history, and time data stay in your browser. (Favicons load from public icon services.)
